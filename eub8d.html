THE WHISPERING WILLOW PROMISE


It is our passion to provide the ultimate experience for our guests. Whispering Willow is a venue that caters to everyone involved, it is our mission to provide an unforgettable experience that captures everything the bride and groom ever dreamed of.


THE WHISPERING WILLOW EXPERIENCE


Whispering Willow features many exclusive amenities for you to enjoy during your special event. From the estate house, to the pond, and finally our beautiful ballroom barn, Enjoy beautiful sights filled with love and attention to detail.

THE BALLROOM
BARN

Whispering Willow boasts over 92 acres of private land, including a large, majestic ballroom barn which seats up to 400 people. With wonderfully tall ceilings, crystal chandeliers, and beautiful natural light that beams through the windows; The barn is perfect for any style of wedding. From quaint rustic gatherings, to the most glamorous modern ceremonies and receptions.

THE COCKTAIL
PASTURE

As you celebrate your recent nuptials, you can join your guests in the cocktail pasture for drinks and celebratory treats while your reception space is styled to your reception party's specifications.

THE
POND

An elegant ceremony set up overlooking a pond. Imagine exchanging vows by this gorgeous view surrounded by majestics trees, a beautiful green lawn and all your loved ones. Your guests will be blown away by this charming view.

THE BALLROOM
BARN

Fashioned after an 1850's antebellum mansion - The Sandlewood Manor House is the architectural Pièce de Résistance of the Sandlewood Estate. A stunning location for a lively cocktail hour, posh rehearsal dinner or intimate ceremony.

THE SANDLEWOOD
CHAPEL

Awe-inspiring chandeliers, floor to ceiling black granite enclosed fireplaces and a bar that would make the Great Gatsby jealous - The Sandlewood Manor Grand Ballroom is ten-thousand square feet of elegance and luxury.
