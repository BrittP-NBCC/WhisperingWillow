Ballroom Barn

Whispering Willow boasts over 92 acres of private land, including a large, majestic ballroom barn which seats up to 240 people.

The barn has wonderfully tall ceilings, crystal chandeliers, and beautiful natural light that beams through the windows. The barn is perfect for any style of wedding, from quaint, rustics gatherings to the most glamorous and moderns ceremonies and receptions.

Cocktail Pasture

As you celebrate your recent nuptials, you can join your guests in the cocktail pasture for drinks and celebratory treats. Take a moment to enjoy your guests and take in the day in our lovely pastures equipped with tables and chairs designed to match your theme.

The Pond

An elegant ceremony set up overlooking a serene pond. Imagine exchanging vows by this gorgeous view surrounded by majestic trees, beautiful greenery and all your loved ones. Your guests will be blown away by this charming view.
