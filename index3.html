Whispering Willow

AN ELEGANT WEDDING & EVENTS VENUE IN SCOUDOUC, NB

Whispering Willow is an elegant Wedding & Events Venue in Scoudouc, New-Brunswick, offering exquisite wedding ceremonies, receptions, and special events on more than 92 gorgeous acres of beautifully landscaped grounds. You will be swept away as you enter a breathtaking Luxury Estate featuring a grand ballroom barn, a romantic outdoor cocktail PASTURE complete with a flaming fire pit, an acre fountain pond with deck and gazebo, a luxurious bridal suite and state-of-the-art groom's quarters, five mansion suites for overnight accommodations, and a comfortable bar lounge perfect for families to enjoy cocktails and relax as the bridal party prepares for the wedding.

THE WHISPERING WILLOW PROMISE

Whispering Willow was built upon the premise that our couples should never have to settle. We created the ultimate venue that caters to everyone involved, including the bride, the groom, parents, grandparents, and every guest. It is the mission of Whispering Willow to provide an unforgettable wedding day that encompasses all that the bride and groom desire.

  • Professional and experienced staff to help with everything you may need
  • Assistance in preparing the perfect proposal or wedding day choreography
  • We offer an array of custom bridal and bridesmaid's bouquets, boutonnieres, and centrepieces of all shapes and sizes to fit your style, colour palette, and theme.

We look forward to working with you on theme & concept development, tabletop designs, florals and more

THE WHISPERING WILLOW EXPERIENCE


Stepping on to our property is like stepping into a hidden gem - an elegant, intimate and serene location. It is our passion to provide the ultimate experience for our guests and to provide an unforgettable experience that captures everything a couple could ever dream of.

The Ballroom Barn
Whispering Willow boasts over 92 acres of private land, including a large, majestic ballroom barn which seats up to 400 hundred people. The barn has wonderfully tall ceilings, crystal chandeliers, and beautiful natural light that beams through the windows. The barn is perfect for any style of wedding, from quaint, rustic gatherings to the most glamourous and modern ceremonies and receptions.
The Dream House & Gazebo
During your stay, you’ll enjoy the comfort of an entire house to yourselves, complete with a lavish bridal suite featuring a Jacuzzi tub, a stylish Groom’s Room including a well stocked bar, three gorgeous bedrooms, a kitchen equipped with your foods of choice, and a spacious living area.
Adjacent to the house is a large gazebo that can easily fit 10-15 individuals, perfect for your wedding brunch or rehearsal dinner.
The Bridal Suite
The Bridal Suite’s crowning jewel is the grand Jacuzzi tub, where you can relax with your coffee in the morning or wind down in the evening with your Complimentary Champagne. The spacious open layout is ideal for hosting your bridesmaids as you enjoy getting ready. The bridal suite balcony is the perfect spot for viewing the sun as it sets across the pond.
The Groom’s Room
During your stay, you’ll enjoy the luxurious Groom’s Room, including a well stocked bar and a billiard table, this spacious room exudes a classic cool, and is the perfect place to relax with your groomsmen as you get ready for your big day.
The Makeup Suite
The Makeup Suite, adjacent to the Bridal Suite, is a makeup artist’s dream, featuring state-of-the-art, high-tech lighting. You will love getting pampered here on your special day.
Cocktail Paddock
As you celebrate your recent nuptials, you can join your guests in the cocktail paddock for drinks and celebratory treats, while your reception space is styled to your specifications for your reception party.
Nature
Whispering Willow is home to a very large variety of birds native to New Brunswick. Most days, all you hear is their song, nature’s medley. You can find geese, ducks, blue herons, pheasants, deer and rabbits at Whispering Willow.
